--- title: Rails layout: rails ---

A backend focused web development framework for Ruby. Best for multi page applications, and provides a built in layer for abstracting database operations with ActiveRecord.

Follows the MVC structure, is fairly opinionated about how your project should be structured and provides a lot of helpers to help you get to a minimum viable product faster. Has a great ecosystem of useful gems to cover more or less anything you could want to do.

On the other hand, its high level of abstraction/ability to solve most problems by just installing a gem mean it's less performant than some frameworks. But not to a level that's likely to be relevant to anything I make for a while.

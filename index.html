About Me

I have professional experience in web development with Ruby on Rails on AWS, and an interest in any and all areas of programming. Though I chose to start with web development due to plentiful high-quality free learning resources I would love to get experience in other areas. For example, hearing people constantly praise Rust makes me want to use it for something, and developing embedded software within strict performance constraints seems like it would be an interesting challenge which would suit my appetite for endless optimization.

I began learning to code in February 2022 and in November 2022 joined KidsUP (my current employer) as an English teacher. Shortly after joining the company, I was entrusted with sole responsibility for designing, developing and organizing hosting for a new event registration site to replace the incumbent Google Forms/Sheets arrangement. That site is currently in production use for KidsUP's biggest seasonal event of the year, with more than 3 000 bookings expected by the end of the event, and I have been given responsibility for multiple other projects related to modernizing internal systems.

This trial by fire has certainly been a valuable learning experience and greatly accelerated my progress as a developer. To take the next step in that progress I am currently exploring opportunities to join a more traditionally structured development team, with the aim of filling the gaps left in said progress by the unusual circumstances of my experience thus far. If you're interested in hiring a full stack web developer with a proven ability to quickly learn and improve under pressure, feel free to get in touch via any of the communication channels found in the Contact section below!

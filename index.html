About Me

Welcome to my portfolio site! I'm Brett, an aspiring developer with professional experience in web development using Ruby on Rails, but an interest in any and all areas of programming.

I began learning to code in February 2022 and in November 2022 joined KidsUP (my current employer) as an English teacher. Shortly after joining the company, I was entrusted with sole responsibility for designing, developing and organising hosting for a new event registration site to replace the incumbent Google Forms/Sheets arrangement. That site is currently in production use for KidsUP's biggest seasonal event of the year, with more than 3 000 bookings expected by the end of the event.

While this trial by fire was certainly a valuable learning experience and greatly accelerated my progress as a developer, I am currently exploring opportunities to join a more traditionally structured development team with the aim of filling the gaps left in said progress by the unusual circumstances of my experience thus far. If you're interested in hiring a junior full stack web developer with a proven ability to quickly learn and improve under pressure, feel free to get in touch via any of the communication channels found in the Contact section below!
